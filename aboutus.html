Welcome...

Ever had the urge to write about your college life? To give your thoughts and experiences the wings of flight to soar and meet the world? If so, we built the perfect home for such urges - a college blog, where you can share and educate yourself with the opinions and experiences of your peers. This is one place for all adventures, talks, experiences and ideas. Here by, we provide you, the students and faculty of B.M.S College of Engineering a modern looking platform to share their perspectives, opinions, ideas, adventures and all the other stuff that life has had in store for you. We encourage all the students and faculty to come be a part of this beautiful platform and build a vivid and varied community online.
