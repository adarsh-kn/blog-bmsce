Outreach Program.

Kaushal B  |  1BM17BT034

Oct 18, 2019

   Dr. Saisha .V & Prathibha were resource persons (on Foldscope) for an Outreach program on "Workshop on Basic Techniques in Biology" at Indian Academy Degree College Autonomous, Bangalore on 17th October 2019.

   The purpose of the Out-reach programme is to reach out Science to the school students to help them appreciate Science in an enthusiastic way. With this aim, Departments of Biochemistry, Microbiology and Zoology, Indian Academy Degree College Autonomous (IADCA) & Department of Biotechnology, BMS College of Engineering in association with Indian Women Scientists Association, Bengaluru Branch organized a workshop on “Basic Techniques in Biology” on 17th October 2019 for the 9th and 10th Std students of The Banyan School.
