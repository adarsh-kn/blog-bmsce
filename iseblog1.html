   The VI Semester students of the Department of Information Science and Engineering visited the BSNL Network Operation Centre on 3rd April. 19 students participated in the visit. The session commenced at 10.30 with an interactive session followed by an introduction to the telecommunication industry by Mr. Chandran. The session further proceeded from the history of the sector to currently implemented technologies used in various sections of the market to provide adequate support to its customers.
